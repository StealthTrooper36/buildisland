Foreign Relations

Learn about Aetheria's diplomatic ties, alliances, and interactions with other nations.

Diplomatic Overview

As a proud nation, Aetheria maintains respectful and cooperative relationships with countries around the world. Our diplomatic efforts are aimed at fostering peace, trade, and cultural exchange.

Our Allies

We work closely with several nations to ensure mutual prosperity and security. Our key allies include:

  • Novara: A nation known for its technological advancements.
  • Veridia: A close partner in trade and environmental initiatives.
  • Draconia: An ally in security and defense matters.

Trade Relations

Aetheria is committed to fair and sustainable trade practices. We are continuously negotiating trade agreements that benefit our economy and our international partners.
