Berezaa kills off VIP commands

Published on: Monday, 6th January 2025 (happy new year xd)

Published by: Eunoia Studios (Capris_bestman)

Yesterday, on the 5th of January, Berezaa announced a new update on Build island. This update added a few fixes and blocks, however it removed a lot of beloved commands previously accessed with the “VIP” gamepass.

This update was received very negatively, as not only did it open source all modkits, but it also removed a lot of VIP commands, registering the gamepass as practically useless unless you want a yellow nametag.

(Modkit owners may drop out of this open source program, but in exchange their modkit will be removed from the game)

After the update was announced, it started receiving a lot of backlash from the community, and people started to spam “GIVE ME REFUND” in the general chat.

Even modkit owners also believe that the update to modkits is stupid.

After the update, Blockdogcat (the owner of the blockdogcat modkit) got his modkit removed from the game due to a joke he made. (mf got the capris bestmen treatment LOL)

Overall, the update was received very negatively, and most people believe it should be reverted.
