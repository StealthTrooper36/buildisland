Published on: Saturday, 2nd November 2024

Yesterday, URSA declared war on USM under the order of the SMF, the reason is to reclaim parts of Hardastan which was stolen by the Mythrans during the first Koshkavian-URSAN war.

Today we started on the third stage of our Hadarstan plan, where we retake and liberate parts of Hadarstan that have been taken from us in the Mythran War. These parts of our interest are quite small on the grand scale, though we still have to deal with fierce opposition.

We are leading this last part on direct orders of the SMF, As it is the goal of the coalition to liberate as much of Mythra as pssible. Together we will make this possible and kill this imperalist, and re-establish the full URSAN nation.
